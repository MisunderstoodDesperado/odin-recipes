Boiled Potatoes

Boil em

Samwise said to boil your potatoes, will you?

Ingredients

  • 2lbs red or yellow potatoes
  • 3 tablespoods unsalted butter
  • 3 tablespoods extra virgin olive oil
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • 3 tablespoods parsley

Directions

  1. Wash and scrub potatoes and cut larger potatoes in half so potato pieces are even-sized.
  2. Place potatoes in a pot of salted water and cover with an inch of water. Bring to a boil then reduce heat and simmer until potatoes are fork tender (10-18 mintues depending on size).
  3. Drain off the water and return potatoes to the pot (or to a mixing bowl).
  4. Drizzle melted butter and olive oil over potatoes.
  5. Season with kosher salt and black pepper, adding both to taste.
  6. Garnish with freshly chopped parsley and toss to combine.
